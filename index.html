The Chrome extension that judges your 47 open tabs so you don't have to.

Opening a new browser tab is a subtle but real increase in cognitive load. What starts as a simple action quickly turns into dozens of open tabs that are easy to forget and hard to manage. Most tab management tools respond with metrics and dashboards. Jesty responds with personality.

Every time you open a new tab, Jesty reads your tab titles, picks a mood, and delivers a roast tailored to your current browser chaos. Entertainment-first with a productivity wrapper. The product is the joke. The character is the product. The user is the punchline.

Target: Tab hoarders who find it funny, not stressful. Personality-driven product enthusiasts. Social sharers. Procrastinators with a sense of humor.
Category: Entertainment / browser personality
Stack: Chrome Extension (Manifest V3) · OpenAI GPT-4o-mini · DM Sans · SVG characters
